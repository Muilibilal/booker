How It Works

Registration and Login:
To use the package booking system, you need to create an account by registering with your personal details. Once registered, you can log in to the system using your email and password.
Booking a Package:
To send a package, you provide the necessary details such as the sender's and recipient's information, package size, weight, and any specific instructions. The system may offer different shipping options, allowing you to choose the service level that suits your needs, such as standard, priority, or economy. After reviewing the details, you confirm the booking, and the system generates a unique tracking number for your package.
Tracking a Package:
With the tracking number provided, you can track the progress of your package throughout its journey. You can visit the package tracking page on the website and enter the tracking number to get real-time updates on the status and location of your shipment. The system will provide information about pickup, transit, and delivery milestones, helping you stay informed about your package's whereabouts.

Payment:
The system will present you with the payment options for the shipping service you selected. You can choose your preferred payment method, such as credit card or digital wallet, and securely make the payment online. Once the payment is processed, your booking is considered complete, and your package is ready for pickup.

Delivery:
When your package reaches its destination, the recipient will be notified for delivery. The system may send notifications via email or SMS to keep you updated on the estimated delivery date and any changes in the delivery schedule. Upon delivery, the recipient may be required to provide a signature or other confirmation to ensure the safe receipt of the package. can log in to the system using your email and password.
